About us
At EnviroParcel, we envision a world where convenience and sustainability seamlessly coexist. Our purpose is to confront the urgent environmental challenge posed by e-commerce packaging waste, and we welcome you to become part of this transformative expedition.
E-commerce packaging waste, driven by the convenience of online shopping, is a growing environmental concern. It affects both society and the ecosystem, contributing to greenhouse gas emissions, releasing toxins, and posing health risks through the creation of microplastics in the food chain.
