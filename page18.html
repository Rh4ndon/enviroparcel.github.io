Innovation 
Programs
  • There is a Reusability Program that offers high-quality services for managing and reclaiming reusable packaging, customizing solutions for businesses looking to shift from disposable to reusable options.

  • Guidelines for Ecommerce Sellers: These guidelines aim to encourage minimalistic and eco-friendly packaging practices.

  • Benefits of Optimizing Packaging Design: Optimizing packaging design results in a reduced environmental impact, less waste, and the use of eco-friendly materials. It also leads to lower production costs, efficient material usage, and reduced storage costs, ultimately enhancing the brand image by reflecting a commitment to eco-friendly practices.

  • Exploring alternative materials and printing processes, such as mushroom packaging and water-based inks, companies are taking steps to reduce emissions and pollutants. These efforts contribute to a reduction in plastic usage, minimizing reliance on traditional plastics and subsequently lowering plastic waste.
