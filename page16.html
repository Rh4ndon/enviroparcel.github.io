Optimizing Packaging Design
Manufacturers in the Philippines are adapting packaging sizes to meet consumer needs, with examples in the food and beverage industry and the beauty and personal care sector.

These changes in packaging size strategies are driven by consumer convenience and cost-effectiveness. Optimization of packaging really helps us a lot because it decreases the amount of waste we generate plus it is also more economical for it uses less amount of material that may cause to lesser cost for packaging,
One example are the water bottles that uses shorter cap and lesser amount of plastic, we know that there are no other alternatives yet for plastic bottles that use to contain water but we find ways to help the environment.

Optimizing packaging design leads to reduced environmental impact it means less waste, and utilizing eco-friendly materials. Lower production costs, efficient material use, reduced storage costs. And it enhanced the brand image which portrays a commitment to eco-friendly practices.
