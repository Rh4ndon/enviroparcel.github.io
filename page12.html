Statistics of E-Commerce
In 2018, the recycling rate for containers and packaging, particularly corrugated boxes, was 53.9%. Combustion with energy recovery accounted for 7.4 million tons (21.5% of total), and landfills received 30.5 million tons (20.9% of total landfilling). Materials in these products include paper, paperboard, glass, steel, aluminum, plastics, wood, and others. The data spans from 1960 to 2018, tracking tons generated, recycled, composted, combusted, and landfilled. The provided references cover various aspects of reducing packaging waste and the role of reusable packaging in promoting sustainability in different industries.
