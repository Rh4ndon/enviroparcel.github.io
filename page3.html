Environmental Law in the Philippines:
Ecological Solid Waste Management Act (RA 9003)
  • This legislation outlines the guidelines for managing solid waste in the Philippines, encompassing aspects such as recycling and disposal. It promotes the reduction of waste and the adoption of appropriate waste disposal methods.
