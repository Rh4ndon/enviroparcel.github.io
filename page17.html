Innovative Design for packages
Manufacturers in the Philippines are adapting packaging sizes to meet consumer needs, with examples in the food and beverage industry and the beauty and personal care sector.
The adoption of these innovative packaging designs is gradually gaining momentum, with businesses recognizing the long-term advantages they offer. The biodegradability and use of eco-friendly materials in these designs serve to further decrease their environmental impact, aligning with sustainability objectives and consumer expectations
There are a lot of businesses in the Philippines that are implementing innovative packaging designs such as the Kani Sushi Bake Overload where its container is reusable and perfect for its size.

This is indeed an effective solution. Innovative packaging design has the potential to substantially lessen the dependence on conventional plastics, resulting in a reduction in the production of plastic waste. This reduction is crucial for alleviating the environmental consequences linked to plastic pollution and waste.
