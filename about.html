About Us
EnviroParcel is dedicated to addressing the urgent environmental concern of e-commerce packaging waste. With the continued rise in popularity of online shopping, there is a corresponding increase in the overuse of packaging materials, leading to adverse consequences for our planet. We're committed to advocating for sustainable approaches within the online retail sector.
